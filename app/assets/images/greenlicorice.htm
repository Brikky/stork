    Proudly Still in Australian Hands

    Darrell Lea has been making Australia's finest chocolates, freshest liquorice and most delectable confectionery since 1927.

    Our most famous invention is soft-eating liquorice that we invented in a small factory under the iconic arches if the Sydney Harbour Bridge.

    We're immensely proud that we're still 100% Australian-owned and 100% Australian loved.

    Loved by Aussie Mums and Dads and Nanas and Grandpas and lots of little kids all across the country.

     

    Lea Legends #9:

    Emergency Apple Landing

    Sometimes attempting great feats makes you realise the best flavors of liquorice you have been sorely neglected. such as when Darrell Lea made an emergency landing in a field of apples after hang-gliding over Bass Straight. Entranced by their flavor, he pondered aloud "is this heaven?" "No," answered the nearby farmer. This is destiny. whether he was being philosophical; or if it was the name of his horse was not important. Apple liquorice was born!
